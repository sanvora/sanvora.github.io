Brief Bio

I am a seasoned engineer with a wide range of knowledge and skills spanning multiple disciplines. As some may say, a jack-of-many-trades and a master of some. I can perform electrical and mechanical engineering tasks as well as perform IT duties and fulfill a developer role.


Throughout my career, I have worked with data in numerous ways. I have conducted experiements and research on thermistor inks and generated models with high accuracy in prediction. I have performed multitudes of test, measurement, and data collection to control manufacturing process and improve designs. Currently, my interest is working with sensors, radars, lidars, and computer vision applying machine learning and narrow AI.


As of December 2019, I have obtained a Master of Science in Business Analytics and Information Systems. (A curriculum that focuses on the many facets of software development and system design as well as statistics, analytics, data science, machine learning, deep learning.) Data/Information has great power. And with great power... (well, you know the rest.) I look forward to using that knowledge to do something for good for the world.
